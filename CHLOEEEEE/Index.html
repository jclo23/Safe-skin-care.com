Skincare Tips for Different Skin Types

Sensitive Skin

Sensitive skin is when you have a bad reaction to cosmetic products. Here’s the routine:

  • Morning: Cleanser, Toner, Antioxidant Serum, Hydrating Serum, Eye Cream, Moisturizer, Sunscreen
  • Night: Oil-Based Cleanser, Water-Based Cleanser, Toner, Targeted Serum, Eye Cream, Retinol, Moisturizer

Normal Skin

Normal skin may come down to genetics. Here's your skincare routine:

  • Morning: Cleanser, Toner, Antioxidant Serum, Hydrating Serum, Eye Cream, Moisturizer, Sunscreen
  • Night: Oil-Based Cleanser, Water-Based Cleanser, Toner, Targeted Serum, Eye Cream, Retinol, Moisturizer

Combination Skin

Combination skin is when you have both oily and dry areas. Try this routine:

  • Morning: Balanced Cleanser, Toner, Antioxidant Serum, Hydrating Serum, Eye Cream, Oil-Free Moisturizer, Sunscreen
  • Night: Oil-Based Cleanser, Water-Based Cleanser, Toner, Targeted Serum, Eye Cream, Retinol, Moisturizer

Oily Skin

Oily skin is common and presents unique challenges. Follow this regimen:

  • Morning: Foaming Cleanser, Toner, Antioxidant Serum, Lightweight Hydrating Serum, Eye Cream, Oil-Free Moisturizer, Sunscreen
  • Night: Oil-Based Cleanser, Water-Based Cleanser, Toner, Salicylic Acid Serum, Eye Cream, Retinol, Oil-Free Moisturizer

Sustainability: Eco-Friendly Practices & Cruelty-Free Products

Discover how sustainable skincare is better for you and the planet.

Eco-Friendly Practices & Packaging

Sustainable skincare brands use ethically sourced, organic, and biodegradable ingredients to reduce environmental impact. Packaging is made from recyclable materials like glass, aluminum, or cardboard, cutting down on plastic use.

These brands promote sustainable routines with biodegradable packaging, ensuring less waste and pollution.

Cruelty-Free Products

Cruelty-free practices go hand-in-hand with eco-conscious efforts. Brands committed to sustainability ensure their products are not tested on animals, advocating for a compassionate, ethical industry.

Why Sustainable Skincare Matters

Sustainable skincare is great for sensitive or allergy-prone skin due to its gentler, natural ingredients. It's also environmentally friendly: plant-based, biodegradable ingredients break down quickly, reducing pollution when rinsed down the drain.

Unlike conventional products that contain harmful chemicals, sustainable brands reduce water contamination and protect ecosystems.
